The aims and objectives of the club shall comprise -

  1. Organising Mathematical Festivals and Informatics Festivals annually.
  2. Organising Intra-School/College Mathematics and Informatics Olympiads, Events, and Competitions.
  3. Celebrating historically significant days in Mathematics.
  4. Participating in National Mathematical Festivals, Events, and Olympiads.
  5. Hosting seminars and workshops on mathematics and topics related to mathematics.
  6. Publishing mathematical magazines and wall magazines regularly.
